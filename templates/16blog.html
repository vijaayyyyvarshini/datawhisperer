My ears have heard this line uncountable times, and I have just nodded and let loose of the sentence.

My past week was indeed a rollercoaster. I went up and down and up again.

I had such mood swings and was emotionally drained that I did not dare to open LinkedIn and keep up with trends and news.

Maybe, this is the break my brain needed, and it overrides the days when I denied the idea of taking a break.

But, life works in its ways and I couldn't stand a chance in front of the tantrums my mental health was weighing down on me.
